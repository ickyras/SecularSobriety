Secular Sobriety is a bi-weekly AA meeting that welcomes all, regardless of belief, background, or identity. The meetings are open, meaning anyone interested in AA's program of recovery is welcome. We strive to provide a space where everyone, especially those who may feel out of place in traditional meetings, can feel seen, heard, and respected.
